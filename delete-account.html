What happens when you delete your account

  • All your study progress (flashcard states, review history, due dates) will be permanently deleted
  • All conversation history from speaking practice and AI tutor will be deleted
  • Your onboarding preferences and account information will be removed
  • This action cannot be undone
  • Deletion is processed within 30 days of your request

You can delete your account directly in the app by going to Profile → Account → Delete Account. If you can't access the app, use the form below to request account deletion.
